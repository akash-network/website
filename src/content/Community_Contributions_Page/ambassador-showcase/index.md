The Akash Student Ambassador Program brings together passionate students from universities around the world who are building in AI, Web3, and decentralized infrastructure. This showcase highlights their contributions across technical projects, community events, content creation, and more.

## Program Highlights

Ambassadors contribute across four key paths: Technical, Events, Content, and Community. From hosting workshops at universities like Cornell, Princeton, and USC to building tools on the Akash Network, student ambassadors are driving adoption of decentralized cloud computing on campuses worldwide.

## Get Involved

The Akash Student Program is open to students at any university. Applications open May 2026. Visit the showcase to see what our ambassadors have built and learn how to join.
